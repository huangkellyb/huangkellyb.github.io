FUDGY BROWNIES

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350ÂºF.
  2. Melt the butter in the microwave, stirring the butter in 10 second intervals until completely melted. This works best if you first chop the butter into smaller pieces.
  3. Whisk together the butter, oil, espresso, and sugar until ALL of the sugar is dissolved.
  4. Beat in the eggs and vanilla for about 1 minute, or until the mixture turns into a lighter creamy color.
  5. Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, salt, and fold in the ingredients until it is JUST combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a lightly greased baking pan. The batter will be thick, so smooth the top out evenly.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup espresso
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
